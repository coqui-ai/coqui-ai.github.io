### At Coqui you will:

- Build and maintain code for Coqui Studio, our web-based AI Voice Studio
- Collaborate with our product and design experts on features and designs
- Collaborate with our ML experts to expose the full power of our TTS models to users
- Work across all levels of our tech stack, from the UI to the TTS models in the backend
- Define the technical architecture for evolving Coqui Studio and exposing new model capabilities
- Mentor other engineers and help design tools and processes for internal collaboration

### About Coqui:

Coqui was founded by the creators of Mozilla’s text-to-speech and speech-to-text engines (over 5M downloads and
30K GitHub stars), has the backing of fantastic investors from around the globe,
has a friendly & diverse developer community, and we’re just getting started.

At Coqui we are redefining how humans interact with computers through that most natural of interfaces, speech.
Our product, Coqui Studio, exposes the full power of our TTS models to creators. We believe that open and free
is better than closed and controlled. So our core text-to-speech engine is open to all, providing an evermore
critical technology, speech, to the world.

Coqui is privately held, founded in 2021, and is headquartered in Berlin. We are a remote-friendly company with lots
of experience working with distributed teams.

We are an equal opportunity employer and value diversity. We do not discriminate on the basis of race, religion,
color, national origin, gender, sexual orientation, age, marital status, veteran status, or disability status.
