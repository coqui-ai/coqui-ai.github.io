Looking for the next challenge, the chance to plan and execute strategies that will excite, engage, and grow a

### At Coqui you will:

- Plan and execute strategies that excite, engage, and grow the developer community
- Write blog posts demonstrating features of Coqui’s technologies
- Create videos demonstrating features of Coqui’s technologies
- Speak at developer conferences on Coqui’s technologies
- Host developer meetups focusing on Coqui’s technologies
- Support Coqui’s developer community on various chat platforms
- Work with ML and Product experts to design, launch, and support developer tools

### Your professional profile:

#### Requirements

- 2+ years relevant experience, e.g. community manager
- 2+ years experience programming (Python, JavaScript…)
- Self-motivated and self-managing, with strong organizational skills
- Strong interpersonal and relationship building skills
- Experience working with a remote team
- Experience with open source projects
- Strong technical communication skills

#### Preferred Qualifications

- Experience with speech technologies, e.g. speech-to-text (STT) and/or text-to-speech (TTS)
- Experience in a startup environment

### About Coqui:

Coqui was founded by the creators of Mozilla’s text-to-speech and speech-to-text engines (over 500K downloads and
22K GitHub stars), has the backing of fantastic investors from around the globe (London, San Francisco, and Berlin),
has a friendly & diverse developer community, and we’re just getting started.

At Coqui we are redefining how humans interact with computers through that most natural of interfaces, speech.

Also we believe that open and free is better than closed and controlled. So our core speech-to-text and text-to-speech
engines are open to all, providing an evermore critical technology, speech, to the world. So when you join Coqui
you have the opportunity to make a dent in the universe, bringing voice to the world.

Coqui is privately held, founded in 2021, and is headquartered in Berlin. We are a remote-friendly company with lots
of experience working with distributed teams.

We are an equal opportunity employer and value diversity. We do not discriminate on the basis of race, religion,
color, national origin, gender, sexual orientation, age, marital status, veteran status, or disability status.
