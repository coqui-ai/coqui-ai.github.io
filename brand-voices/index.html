    A Clone at Your Beck and Call
    (Banded Voice Cloned, and Always on Call)
    Text-to-Speech

    Coqui text-to-speech will let your brand voice talent sleep in. Effortlessly clone the voice of your brand talent and have the clone handle burning the midnight oil: reading breaking news broadcasts in the middle of the night, narrating news stories on your website, or simply reading out the station identification.

    Import

    Import the audio assets, e.g. recordings of your voice talent, into Coqui's pipeline with the click of a button.

    Clone

    With the click of a button, you can clone the voice of your brand voice talent and let them sleep in. The clone can read breaking news broadcasts, narrate website news stories, or any number of other things.

    Direct

    Your cloned voice is also directable. You control the enunciation and emotion; the pitch and prosody; rate, duration, and contour. It's all under your control. You are the director of their performance.

    Deploy

    Whether prototyping or premiering, deployment is a click away, bringing production times down from months to minutes.
