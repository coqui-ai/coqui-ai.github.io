Efficient Transcription of Longer Recordings

By Reuben Morais

The official Coqui STT packages offer best-in-class support for real-time, single-stream, low-latency speech recognition. They‚Äôre highly tuned for fast start-up, low disk space and memory utilization, and low-latency transcription results.
